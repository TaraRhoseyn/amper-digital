About me

Hi, I’m Tara and I’ve been working in digital accessibility across the public and private sectors for 3+ years. I have a proven track record of improving compliance and the overall culture towards accessibility at every workplace.

I was diagnosed with a learning disability at a young age and have been using assistive technologies ever since, prompting a passion for creating inclusive digital user experiences.

My passion, combined with my background in software engineering, led me to becoming an expert in Web Accessibility Content Guidelines (WCAG) and Pubic Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations (PSBAR), to name just a few areas of accessibility!

I offer auditing, strategy and consultancy services to companies looking to improve their accessibility approaches or check their compliance status.
